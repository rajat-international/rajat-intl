About Us

Delivering Quality Fashion with Expertise

Rajat International is a trusted textile manufacturer and exporter since 1989. We make high-quality fabrics and readymade garments with reliable delivery and expert craftsmanship.

Our specialties include hand block printing, hand screen printing, hand embroidery, and custom fabric manufacturing. We proudly accept minimum orders starting from just 1 piece, making us the perfect partner for both small businesses and bulk buyers.

Chairman's Message

At Rajat International, we are committed to delivering high-quality textile products on time. Since 1989, we have earned our customers' trust through quality, reliability, and excellent service.

From fabric sourcing to dyeing, printing, embroidery, stitching, quality checks, packing, and export, we carefully manage every step to ensure the best results.
