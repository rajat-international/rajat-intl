About Our Company

Innovative textile solutions for a stylish tomorrow

Since 1989, Rajat International has established itself as a trusted manufacturer and exporter of premium readymade garments and custom badges. Over more than three decades, we have built our reputation on genuine craftsmanship — blending traditional techniques like hand block printing, hand screen printing, and intricate hand embroidery with modern manufacturing processes.

As a 100% exporter to Japan, we cater to one of the most quality-conscious markets in the world, and meeting those high standards has become one of our greatest strengths. We also offer low minimum order quantities (MOQ), making it easy for both small businesses and large-scale buyers to work with us without compromising on quality or pricing.

Every order, regardless of volume, is handled with the same level of precision and consistency, backed by reliable on-time delivery — because in international trade, timing builds trust. This flexibility and commitment to quality is what has kept our clients with us for years, and continues to inspire us to keep raising our own standards.

Our Journey

A history built on craftsmanship

1989

The Beginning

Rajat International was founded as a manufacturer and exporter of ladies' and men's readymade garments, specializing in hand block printing and hand screen printing.

1990s

Growing Craftsmanship

Expanded expertise into intricate hand embroidery, building a skilled team of craftsmen and strengthening our production capabilities.

2000s

Expanding Facilities

Set up dedicated production units for digital printing and final inspection at our Gurugram headquarters, along with a specialized screen-print and hand block printing unit in Jaipur.

Today

A Trusted Global Partner

Today, Rajat International continues to combine traditional craftsmanship with advanced weaving and dyeing technology to serve clients across the globe with quality and reliability.
